Common Questions

How much can I save on business costs?
Savings potential varies based on your current contracts, usage patterns, and the competitiveness of your existing rates. We provide specific comparison analysis during the initial review, showing you the difference between your current costs and available market rates.
What does the cost review process involve?
Start with WhatsApp conversation about your current services. Share recent bills (we handle data securely). Our team benchmarks against market rates, identifies waste, and presents a prioritized savings plan within 48 hours. You decide what to action.
Do I need to switch all services at once?
No. We present opportunities across all categories. Most clients prioritize by highest savings potential or contract end dates. Energy and payments typically deliver quickest ROI, so those often go first.
How do you make money if there's no cost to me?
Industry-standard provider commissions. This doesn't inflate your costsâ€”providers budget for customer acquisition whether you come directly or through us. Our independence means we only recommend switches that deliver genuine savings. If it doesn't save you money, we don't proceed.
What about exit fees or mid-contract switching?
We factor termination costs into ROI calculations. If long-term savings justify immediate exit, we recommend it. If not, we schedule switches to align with contract end dates and set reminders. Energy contracts often have zero exit fees within certain windowsâ€”we time it accordingly.
